About Proverbs

Proverbs are an integral part of the culture and language of any nation.

In Afghanistan, these short and meaningful sayings have been passed down through generations, carrying wisdom, experience, and deep insights into life and human relationships.

This collection is an effort to preserve and share this valuable treasure...

We hope you enjoy reading and understanding these proverbs and apply them in your life.

Proverb Gallery

آب از آب تکان نمی‌خورد
The water doesn't move from the water.
This proverb means that no significant event or change has occurred.
این ضرب المثل به این معناست که هیچ اتفاق یا تغییر مهمی رخ نداده است.
دیگ به دیگ میگه روت سیاه
The pot says to the other pot, your face is black.
This proverb is used to refer to those who have their own faults and shortcomings but blame others.
این ضرب المثل برای اشاره به کسانی بکار میرود که خودشان عیب و نقص دارند اما دیگران را سرزنش میکنند
با یک گل بهار نمیشه
One flower doesn't bring spring.
This proverb indicates that a single good sign or positive event is not enough to draw a general conclusion.
این ضرب المثل نشان میدهد که یک نشانه ی خوب یا یک اتفاق مثبت برای رسیدن به یک نتیجه کلی کافی نیست
عجله کار شیطانه
Haste is the work of the devil.
This proverb emphasizes the importance of patience and thinking before acting, considering haste to be a cause of mistakes.
این ضرب المثل بر اهمیت صبر و فکر کردن قبل از عمل کردن تاکید دارد و عجله را عامل اشتباهات می داند
